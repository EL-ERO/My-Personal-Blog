About Me

Hey! I'm Ella. Eating healthy and maintaining a proper hygiene has changed my world. It's not just about eating the basic greens it's learning to eat everything in the right proportion and also at the right time. Eating healthy has helped me to gain additional skills like cooking a variety of dishes, both traditional and intercontinental, developing better communication and social skills by meeting people with the same health goals in mind to ask for recipes in person and online. My skin has never felt or looked better in all my years. It helps to build your self-esteem and keep you motivated like some sort of self-discipline tactic developer. Join me in this rewarding journey. It's not a compulsory everyday routine, there will be cheat days but remember it's sole goal is helping to keep you organized. You'll have fun, get help and learn along the way!
